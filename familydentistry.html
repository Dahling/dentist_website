The Family Dentistry You’ve Been Looking For

Our highly skilled staff is qualified to treat all ages and any dental issue that may need attention. At Matthew Sheppard Family Dentistry, we do it all under one roof.

Pediatric Dentistry

We love treating kids! We've become trusted friends with our smallest patients. We teach them that going to the dentist is nothing to be afraid of. Instilling good oral health habits at an early age, we provide excellent preventive care and treat any dental problems that arise.

If your child has difficulty with anxiety, we have “happy air” available to help them relax. Parents and children appreciate our gentle methods. We communicate what to expect every step of the way.

Dentistry for the Whole Family

Prevention

Preventive care and maintenance remain vital to good oral health as we age. The prevention of tooth decay and gum disease through brushing, flossing, and regular dental visits will keep your mouth healthy for a lifetime.

Cavities

Once tooth decay starts, it is important to address it quickly. We will clean out the decay and use fun colored, tooth-colored, or safe silver fillings to keep your tooth intact. If a cavity has infected the pulp or is causing you pain, a root canal may be needed. A root canal can save a damaged tooth by removing the pulp, filling it, and covering it with a crown for strength and appearance.

Extractions and Tooth Replacement

When teeth cannot be saved, or if your mouth is crowded, we do extractions, including surgical extractions. Extractions are also done to prepare for dental implants or dentures. Replacing missing or extracted teeth is very important to retain the integrity of your mouth and jaw.

It is not just a cosmetic issue. You need to be able to chew and speak. We are implant specialists. We are able to both surgically place and restore dental implants. We also craft beautiful dentures and bridges to replace some or all of your teeth. You will get brand new replacement teeth!

Cosmetic Solutions

Sometimes after years of wear and tear, our smiles do not look their best. Drinking coffee, red wine, tea, or smoking can stain your teeth. If your teeth are yellowed or discolored, two great options to brighten your smile are teeth whitening and veneers.

Whitening, or bleaching, removes surface stains and increases the brightness of your smile. Veneers are thin shells of porcelain or other tooth-colored material that can change the appearance and color of your teeth. Veneers can also hide gaps, chips, cracks, and even minor alignment issues. We have many cosmetic solutions to improve the look of your smile!
