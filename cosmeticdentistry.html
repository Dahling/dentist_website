    • Cosmetic Dentistry

    It is common to think cosmetic dentistry is just for Hollywood people. The truth is, everyone deserves to have a smile they can be proud of. With the latest technology, one of those people with the beautiful “pearly whites” could be you!

    We know having a healthy mouth and strong teeth are most important. But when treating the whole person, we know that appearance can make a big difference for our patients. If you are embarrassed to laugh or smile, it can definitely affect your confidence and self-esteem.

    You do not have to live in Hollywood to want to look great for your job or have a great smile to flash at your significant other. More and more people are realizing that cosmetic dentistry is not about vanity or luxury – it is about looking and feeling your best every day. We have several options to give you a little boost or make a big change for the better!


    Professional Teeth Whitening

    We offer in-office as well as at-home teeth whitening options. We use OpalEscence professional whitening kits that you can take home. We will give you the right concentration of gel to meet your needs, and fit you with a custom whitening tray. The length of treatment depends on how much whitening you need or desire. Our in-office teeth-whitening, Philips Zoom! WhiteSpeed, uses a special light to activate a special bleaching gel that can improve the brightness of your teeth by up to eight shades. This is completed in less than an hour in one simple visit in our office. You will love your stunning results!
