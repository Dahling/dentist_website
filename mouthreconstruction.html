Full Mouth Reconstruction


When you know that getting a healthy and attractive mouth will require a lot of dental work, it is easy to get overwhelmed and discouraged. The prospect of countless dental appointments and unknown cost is not appealing to anyone. We have good news. If you have multiple dental problems that have bothered you for a long time, there is hope.

Using a compassionate and coordinated approach, we can help assess and create a treatment plan to remedy and repair the problems in a shorter amount of time. When we plan ahead, we can do several procedures at each visit.

You do not have to let anxiety or fear keep you from getting the smile you need and want. We have sedation options that make the process so much easier for you. It will take commitment and follow-through in our partnership to complete your full mouth restoration and reconstruction, but we guarantee it will be worth it!

What is Full Mouth Reconstruction?

It is the process of simultaneously restoring or rebuilding all of your teeth in both the upper and lower jaws. Each case is different and will require numerous visits and procedures to complete. Although there are cosmetic benefits to restoring your whole mouth, it is not considered elective because it is based on need. You need to be able to chew and speak. The fact that the appearance of your smile will improve is just a bonus!

You are a candidate for full mouth reconstruction if:

You have lost teeth due to decay or trauma

You have broken or damaged teeth

Your teeth are worn down from erosion or teeth grinding

You have ongoing complaints of headaches or jaw pain related to your bite

The Reconstruction Process

We will start with a complete examination of your teeth, gums, temporomandibular joint (TMJ), bite, and the overall appearance of your mouth. We will take X-rays, impressions, and photographs of your mouth to develop a comprehensive treatment plan.

Each treatment plan is different from patient to patient, but a full mouth rehabilitation could take a while to complete. It will often require many visits and procedures. We will go over the proposed treatment plan with you, step by step, making sure you understand the time and costs involved.

Dentures


A full mouth reconstruction is quite an undertaking. It will require a major commitment on your part, but we will meet your commitment with ours to see you through the process until it is complete. You will have strong teeth, healthy gums, and a beautiful smile at the end of your journey.
